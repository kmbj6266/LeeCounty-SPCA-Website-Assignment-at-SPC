Services & Resources

We offer a wide variety of services.

Spaying or neutering your pets is the law in the Lee County, as well as other parts of the state of Florida. By spaying or neutering your pets, you reduce the risk of certain cancers and curb negative behaviors like marking, roaming, and mood swings associated with heat cycles.

Behavior Help - If you have an animal behavior question, please call our FREE Behavior Helpline at (888) 772-2521, ext 260

Dog Training - Work with Lee SPCA's expert trainers to set you and your dog up for a lifetime of happiness. Our techniques focus on positive reinforcement and fun with your dog! We offer basic obedience for dogs and puppies, as well as agility classes (for the novice or the competitor), specialty classes like Fun Nose Work, Come When Called, Trick Training, and more!

Solutions for Reactive and Aggressive Dogs - A two-day training seminar with expert trainer Ali Brown for Trainers and Owners of Reactive Dogs Alike Does your dog bark when people or other animals walk by on the street? Do you get nervous when you have new people to your house because you're unsure of what your dog might do? We know how you feel, but you don't have to live with this behavior forever! Whether you're a trainer looking for more information on aggressive or reactive dogs or a dog owner who just needs a little help, come to the Lee SPCA's Scaredy Dog Seminars to learn first-hand from an expert trainer who has been offering classes on easing aggressive canine behavior for over 12 years.

Medical Emergency - If your pet is experiencing a medical emergency, please call your veterinarian immediately. Please note, information contained on this site is for reference only and is not meant to replace visits to a veterinarian or professional trainer.
