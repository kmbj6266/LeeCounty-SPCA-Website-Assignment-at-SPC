Blessing of the Animals

This special service will include a procession of the animals for blessing by Sr. Minister Stephen Bauman. Lee SPCA will also collect new pet toy donations for shelter cats and dogs.
When: Sunday, June 30, 2013, at 2:00 P.M.
Where: Christ Church 
             123 Park Ave., Ft. Meyers, FL 10000
For more information, please contact (555) 123-4567

Bark in the Park!

On Sunday, April 21, the Tampa Bay Rays will hold the 2nd annual "Bark in the Park" at Tropicana Field in downtown St. Petersburg. Fans that purchase a special event ticket in the Coca-Cola All-You-Can-Eat Right Field Pavilion are invited to bring their dogs to the park and help cheer on their home team as the Rays host the Florida Marlins at 6:10 p.m. Come to the game early at 5:30, for the running of the bases with your dog. All dogs required to remain on a leash for the entire attendance at the park.

Adoption Day!

On Saturday, July 13, 2013, our annual tradition of a great event. Come out and enjoy booths, Reggae Music, FREE samples and gifts, lots of fun, and more while finding your new Friend for Life! There will be raffle tickets sold for a $100 gift card to PetSmart. If you bring a donated item from our wish list, you can get valuable coupons to PetSmart.
