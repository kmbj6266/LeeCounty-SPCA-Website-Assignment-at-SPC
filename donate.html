Here are just a few ways your donations help abused and abandoned animals right here in Lee County, FL.

By making a donation, you automatically become a member of our Guest Book

By becoming a member of our Guest Book, you will receive FREE monthly newsletters and email alerts on specials at the SPCA. You will also receive email coupons for valuable pet products. As a bonus, your name will be put into a drawing for a chance to win a $25 Gift Card to PetSmart. One drawing held three times a year.

$25: Helps provide food, shelter and love for a suffering animal.
$50: Gives a motherless kitten a second chance at life.
$75: Provides a full vet exam, vaccinations and microchip to a shelter pet.
$100: Sends 2 shelter dogs to obedience classes, making them more adoptable.
$150: Allows Humane Officers to remove an abused pet from a dangerous situation.
$200: Spays three cats, preventing countless unwanted litters and euthanasia.

WISH LIST: You can also donate much needes items such as: pet beds, paper grocery bags, dog toys, cat toys, collars, cat & dog food, dog & cat treats, newspapers, animal crates, grooming supplies, baby gates, kitty litter, rabbit supplies, cat trees, dog leashes, etc.
