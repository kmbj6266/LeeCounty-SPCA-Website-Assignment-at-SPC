Meet Our Staff



Mark Wilson has been the director of Lee County SPCA since 2000. He is passionate about finding all of our animals new homes. Adam Smith has been the "Pet Hotel Manager" since 2008. He makes sure that all the animals are comfortable, safe and secure.
Dr. Meg has been a veterinarian since 2005, and has been with here since 2010. She grew up tending to horses, dogs, chickens and pigs. Dr. Laura has been a veterinarian since 1995 and has been with here since 2003. She originally moved to Florida to work with retired greyhounds.
Lucy has been the Office Manager since coming here in 2008. She is originally from Alaska. Tom loves training the animals. He is our trainer and volunteer coordinator. He helps facilitate all adoptions and foster care arrangements.
