Fees to Adopt

  • Guinea Pigs & Hamsters $15
  • Parakeets $20
  • Kittens $15
  • Dogs, Cats, & Exotic pets... $Price Varies

Adoption fee includes

  • Spay/neuter surgery (dogs, cats & rabbits)
  • Species-appropriate current vaccinations and microchip (registration not included)
  • Health examination by spcaLA medical staff
  • Certificate for free health examination by participating VCA Animal Hospital
  • Collar

Requirements:

  • Proof of address, Government issued identification
  • Copy of lease and/or landlordâ€™s phone number
  • Payment (cash, Visa and MasterCard only)
  • Family members to meet new pets
  • Adequate time and patience!
The adoption process can take 30 minutes to 1 hour to complete an application and speak with an SPCA Adoption Counselor. Please be Advised: Once an adoption application is approved, you will be required to bring current family pets to meet the new pet. This rule always applies to dogs and sometimes to cats. Adding a new member to your family is a lifetime commitment. Please be sure this is the right decision for you.
