We have many ways that you can volunteer


We need volunteers to walk dogs and play with the animals. Even if you can only commit to one hour a week, this will help the animals feel less stressed and feel loved. You can make a big difference.
We have after school programs for kids and adults to help wash pets. We also have "Pet Wash" day every Saturday from 2:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. Call us to find out what other ways you can volunteer.
Would you like to be a foster parent? Many pets are available to keep as foster pets. This is a great way to find out if your kids are ready for a pet or if you can take on the commitment.
